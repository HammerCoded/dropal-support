Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

Dropal ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Dropal processes all color extraction, palette creation, and wallpaper design entirely on your device.

iCloud Sync

Your palettes, meshes, and wallpapers are synced via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible to us. Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy apply to this data.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • Screen views and feature usage (e.g., which templates are popular)
  • App crashes and performance data
  • Subscription events (purchase, renewal, cancellation)

Analytics data does not include personally identifiable information. We use Apple's App Analytics and may use third-party analytics services that comply with Apple's App Store guidelines.

App Tracking Transparency

Dropal respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. If the app requests tracking permission, you can decline without any impact on functionality. No data is shared with third-party advertisers.

Photos & Camera

Dropal accesses your photo library and camera only when you choose to extract colors from an image or save a wallpaper. Photos are processed on-device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Subscriptions

Dropal Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription management is handled by Apple.

Data Storage

All app data (palettes, meshes, wallpapers, settings) is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud container. We do not operate servers that store your content.

Third-Party Services

Dropal does not share your data with third parties. The only external services used are:

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit) for data sync
  • Apple StoreKit for subscription management

Children's Privacy

Dropal does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at dropal@martellis.app.
